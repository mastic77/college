Roshan International College was established in 2000 AD as a privately owned academic institution located in Koteshwor, kathmandu, Nepal. It is affiliated to Tribhuvan University and approved by University Grants Commission of Nepal. Roshan offers Bachelor Degree programs such as BBS, BCA, BA, and BSW. It has been imparting holistic education with excellent results. The aim of education is to nurture strong-willed, responsible, principled, optimistic, trustworthy, competent, well mannered and humanitarian citizens of the nation and the globe who can learn whatever they need to know in any new context. Candidly speaking, our education recognizes the innate potential of every student for intelligent, creative, systemic thinking and such students believe in democracy, human rights & diversity and possess critical thinking to face the rising challenges of this modern era.
